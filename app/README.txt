Requirements:
- API 33 on your Android Emulator
- Downgrade your Android Emulator to version 31.2.9 (video tutorial here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=qo3SbdbXFos

To run the app:
- Run app in Emulator.
- Sign up for your own account (right-most tab), or log in (left-most tab)
- Click/tap on the name of a store to display the route to that store
- Click/tap on the "Reminders" title or on the checkmark button in the navigation bar to navigate to the RemindersActivity.
- Add a reminder by choosing the destination, and set the time you want to arrive at the store. Then click "Add Reminder" button.
